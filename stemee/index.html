False positive

As applied to the field of anti-malware programs, a false positive occurs when the program mistakenly flags an innocent file as being infected. The term is not confined just to the anti-malware world. It also applies, for example, to anti-spam protection, where it refers to the misidentification of a legitimate e-mail message as spam.
